What is Not For Sale's mission?

Globally, millions of people live in slavery. Not For Sale is a global NGO leading innovative solutions that fight human trafficking and environmental exploitation, with survivor support and prevention programs across several continents. We build social enterprises designed to make social impact profitable.

Together with our global community, we're striving for a world where people and planet function in harmony. A world where no one is for sale. Learn more about our work.
